Covid Severity Predictor

You can upload a Chest X-Ray to see the severity of Lung damage



About The System

This is a web interface for a deep learning model which can predict severity of damage caused to lungs due to Covid. The model takes and X-Ray image of Chest and tries to predict lung opacity. We are also trying to provide a visualisation of which region the model considers as the deciding factor.
